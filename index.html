About Me

I grew up in Rochester, NH and lived there until I was 18. Once I graduated from High School I moved to New Brunswick Canada to get my Bachelor's in Religion from Kingswood University. I had been a Pastor for several years after graduating from University where I was often responisible for the church's website and social media presence. This is where I began to search for ways I could pursue this as a full time or part time way of life. It was something I enjoyed being involved with and wanted to go deeper with it. I am happy to be pursuing this full time now and am excited for the challenge ahead.

I love to spend time with other people, whether at a party, outside, playing games, whatever it might be, if I around others then I am happy. I also enjoy taking pictures of people or our beautiful world.
